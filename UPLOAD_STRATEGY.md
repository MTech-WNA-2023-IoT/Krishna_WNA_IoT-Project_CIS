# Upload Strategy

## Best repo names
- battery-soh-rul-predictor
- hegman-gauge-vision
- ucm-sip-diagnostics-toolkit
- raspberry-pi-sensor-gateway
- network-hardening-audit-lab
- sdr-spectrum-sentinel
- materials-characterization-analysis

## Honest project descriptions you can use
- “Sanitized portfolio reconstruction based on my engineering experience.”
- “Public-safe demonstration project inspired by my work in embedded, networking, and analytics domains.”
- “Original implementation built to showcase methods and workflow, without confidential data.”

## What to avoid
- Avoid naming any confidential employer project.
- Avoid uploading private IP addresses, screenshots, credentials, or internal documents.
- Avoid saying these are exact production systems from your employer.
